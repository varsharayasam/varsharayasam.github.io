About

I'm Srivarsha (Varsha) Rayasam, a student at UCLA studying Psychology and Cognitive Science with a focus on learning, perception, and human-computer interaction.

My work spans user research, machine learning, and academic writing. I'm drawn to questions about how people learn — whether that's studying category learning in neurodevelopmental conditions, exploring how AI is reshaping classrooms, or redesigning the apps people rely on every day.

Outside of research, I'm a trained Carnatic classical singer and an avid reader. I also write — about identity, culture, and the quiet moments in between.
