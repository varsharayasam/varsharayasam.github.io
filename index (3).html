About

I'm Srivarsha (Varsha) Rayasam, a graduating senior at UCLA studying Cognitive Science with a computing emphasis and a minor in Professional Writing. I've worked across product management, program management, data analytics, and UX research — I'm drawn to roles where understanding people and building systems overlap.

My work spans building AI-driven tools at Amazon, leading cross-functional product teams, conducting user research, and studying how people learn. I'm especially interested in AI in education and enterprise settings, and in the ways technology can serve large-scale organizations and nonprofits.

Outside of professional and academic experiences, I love trying novel, unique drinks at coffee shops and reading and writing. Some of my favorite books include Unaccustomed Earth by Jhumpa Lahiri, The Golden Son by Soumya Gouda, The Silent Patient by Alex Michaelides, and Escape from Slavery by Francis Bok.
